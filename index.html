HUD Rent-Subsidized Housing Tenant Associations

Tenants have the Right to Organize:

Tenants have the Right to establish and operate a tenant organization to address issues related to living conditions, activities related to housing, and community development.

A Legitimate Resident Organization recognized by HUD is:

  • Meet Regularly [at least quarterly]
  • Operate Democratically
  • Represent All Residents at the Property
  • Be Independent from the Property Owner

Source:Code of Federal Regulations ยง 964.115
